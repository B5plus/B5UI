Why B5 Plus

Mines don't have time for fragmented supply chains. B5 Plus consolidates the steel, structures, media, and fabrication a producing mine consumes every day — into a single account, a single delivery programme, and a single phone call.

We hold inventory in Tema, run owned logistics across the West African corridor, and now manufacture grinding media domestically. The result: shorter lead times, lower landed cost, and resilient supply against import volatility.
