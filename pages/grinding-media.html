Manufacturing Process

From billet to ball — fully traceable.

PLANT 06 · TEMA
Forging line — automated quench & temper

Billet inspection & charge prep

Each billet is identity-tagged, spectro-tested for chemistry, and weighed before charge. Any deviation rejects the heat.

Forging or centrifugal casting

Forged line: induction-heat to 1,050 °C, hot-roll & close-die forge. High-Cr line: melt at 1,500 °C, centrifugal mould casting.

Heat treatment & quench

Continuous-belt austenitising furnace, polymer quench, then automated double-temper to lock in through-hardness.

QA testing & certification

Hardness profile (surface + 4 depths), drop test, microstructure check, dimensional sort. MTC issued per heat.

Pack & dispatch

1 MT bulk bags or 50 kg drums. Direct ex-Tema pickup, or delivered via our trailer fleet across Ghana, Burkina, Côte d'Ivoire, Mali.
