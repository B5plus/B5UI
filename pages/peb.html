What Are PEBs?

Modern steel structures, pre-planned for speed.

Pre-engineered buildings are modern steel structures designed, fabricated, and assembled using a pre-planned engineering system. Unlike traditional construction, PEB systems are manufactured in a controlled environment and assembled on-site for faster deployment.

The result: lower cost, tighter tolerances, less waste, and project timelines measured in weeks instead of months. They are widely used for:

Industrial facilities
Warehouses
Factories
Logistics hubs
Workshops & storage units

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers to the questions our PEB clients ask most often. Need more? Our engineers respond within one business day.

What is a pre-engineered building?

A pre-engineered building is a steel structure that is designed and fabricated in advance and assembled on-site for faster construction. The whole building — primary frame, secondary framing, cladding, fixings — is engineered as one optimized system, not pieced together from generic stock parts.

How long does it take to build a PEB structure?

PEB buildings significantly reduce construction time compared to traditional methods. Exact timeline depends on project size and complexity, but a typical industrial warehouse can be designed, fabricated and erected in a fraction of the time required for conventional construction.

Are PEB buildings durable?

Yes. They are made from high-quality steel and designed to withstand industrial and environmental stresses — including the heat, humidity and coastal salt-air conditions typical of West Africa. Corrosion-resistant coatings extend service life decades beyond build-out.

Can PEB structures be customized?

Yes — fully customizable in terms of size, layout, span, eave height, bay spacing, cladding type, doors, mezzanines, crane gantries, insulation and finish. Every B5 Plus PEB starts with your operational brief, not a catalogue page.

Where do you deliver?

Across Ghana, West Africa, the wider African continent, and international infrastructure projects. Local Tema fabrication keeps lead times short for the West African region.

Do you handle erection on-site?

Yes. We can supply structure-only for your contractor, or supply-and-erect using our own certified erection crews — including foundation coordination, cladding, and project handover documentation.
